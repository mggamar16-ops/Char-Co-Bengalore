Chef's Pick
The Char Experience

Grilled Over
Live Coal

At Char & Co, the tandoor isn't just equipment — it's the soul of the kitchen. Our pits burn through the day, imparting that unmistakable char and smokiness you won't find anywhere else in Bengaluru.

  • ◆ Slow-marinated proteins, minimum 8 hours
  • ◆ Traditional clay tandoor fired to 450°C
  • ◆ Charcoal sourced from sustainable lychee wood
  • ◆ Zero artificial smoke flavoring — ever
